Refund Policy

Smart Promo LLC adheres to standard financial industry practices regarding refunds and service termination.

Financial Retainers

Retainer fees for virtual CFO, tax, and continuous accounting services are non-refundable once work has commenced for the billing period.

Tax Preparation

Fees for tax preparation and one-off consulting are fully earned upon delivery of the completed documents and cannot be refunded.

Cancellation

You may cancel monthly bookkeeping services with a 30-day notice. Unused prepaid fees beyond the 30-day window will be refunded on a prorated basis.
