Refund Policy

Smart Promo LLC strives to provide transparent and fair services for all our clients.

Consultation Services

Initial discovery and diagnostic calls are provided free of charge. Retainer fees for ongoing marketing and consulting services are generally non-refundable once work has commenced.

Digital Marketing Campaigns

Due to the irreversible nature of digital ad spending to third-party platforms (e.g., Google, Facebook), any ad spend budgets that have already been allocated and utilized cannot be refunded.

Cancellation

If you wish to terminate an ongoing monthly service, a 30-day notice is required. Any unused management fees following the 30-day period may be eligible for a prorated refund, determined on a case-by-case basis.

Disputes & Contact

We value client satisfaction. If you are unsatisfied with our service delivery, please contact us immediately so we can attempt to rectify the situation amicably.
